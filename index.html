We are Future Lab; a full student Formula SAE team aims to develop a world-class electric racing car. With passion and skills, we envision a future when and where all people can access the electromobility at low cost. Therefore, we team up with the diverse background of students to solve this challenging problem. From engineering and management department, you could see the student from these subject devote their full measure of devotion to achieve our unified goal, "Dream Together, Win Together."

About Formula SAE - Japan

Formula SAE Japan (FSAEJ) is an opportunity for students to develop skills for object creation "MONO-ZUKURI", which in turn contributes to the expansion of the Japanese automotive industry. This competition was started in 2003 as a public activity for self-motivated students to cultivate various skills involved with obj20t creation, and as training for those who will one day play critical roles in the future of the automotive industry.
